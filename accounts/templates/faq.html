How to get listed?

There are a few prerequisites for your account to be listed on FediDevs.com

1. Your account must be discoverable and not opt-out of search engine indexing

Your account must be discoverable. Check the checkbox that says "Suggest account to others" at {your_instance}/settings/profile.

Opt-out of search engine indexing checkbox must NOT be checked. The checkbox is on the {your_instance}/preferences/other page:

2. Your bio has to have at least one of the filter keywords

3. Have at least one post/toot or one follower or follow a single other account

Accounts with 0 posts, 0 followers, and 0 following are not indexed because they are usually spam accounts.

If you did all the steps above and still don't see your account on FediDevs.com, reach out and we'll check!

How often is the data refreshed?

The data is refreshed about once per week, but if you want your profile on FediDevs.com updated sooner, send us an email and we'll update it manually.

How to get removed from FediDevs.com?

The easiest way is to mark your account as not discoverable in the Mastodon profile settings. Uncheck the checkbox that says "Suggest account to others" at {your_instance}/settings/profile. This will remove your account on next sync (in about 1 week).

If you don't want to wait until the next sync please reach out and we'll remove you immediately.

How to get your instance removed from FediDevs.com?

If you are an admin of an instance that is listed above, but don't want us from indexing it anymore, please send us an email. Once I confirm your identity I will remove all accounts from your instance and never index your instance again.
